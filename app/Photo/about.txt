Photo v1.0

About App:
Photo is a simple and efficient gallery application designed to help you organize and view your photos.

Features:
• View photos by Folder or by Tags.
• Swipe between Folder and Tag views.
• View photos in fullscreen with zoom support.
• Edit photo metadata (rename, tags).
• Organize photos (move, delete, create folders).
• Check for updates directly from the app.
